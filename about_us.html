Dr. Farah Deeba

Dr. Farah Deeba, Associate Professor,Department of Clinical Psychology, University of Dhaka is a lead of this online service for Bangladeshies. She is one of the members of the new generation professional clinical psychologists, working to deal with mental health problems of Bangladeshies of different age since 2002. She mainly works with anxiety, depression and stress.  Her work is focused on children and women issues. She had been employed by the joint project of Bangladesh and Danish governments to develop counselling services for women victims of violence in country from 2006 to 2009. She completed her PhD in Psychology from Macquarie University, Sydney, Australia. Her field of study was mental health of traumatized children and evaluating a very low-cost intervention them in low-resourced countries. She is working for developing clinical psychology service development.


Jesan Ara

Jesan Ara, Assistant Professor and Clinical psychologist at University of Rajshahi. After completion of her HSC from Kushtia Govt. College, Jesan Studied psychology in her undergrad at University of Dhaka. She then enrolled at Department of Clinical Psychology, University of Dhaka. She completed her MS and MPhil degree and successfully joined to Rajshahi University as a faculty of Psychology Department. She also works with various range of mental health problems. She rigorously worked with autistic parents during her MPhil research work. Currently she is doing her PhD in Clinical Psychology on treating depression with cognitive behavior therapy at the Department of Clinical Psychology University of Dhaka.
